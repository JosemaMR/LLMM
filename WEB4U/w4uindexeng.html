Introduction to Markup Languages

Markup languages are used to structure, organize, and present content on the web. Unlike programming languages, they do not perform calculations or logic; instead, they describe information and how it should be displayed.

On this site you will find information and examples of three essential markup languages:

HTML

SVG

CSS
